ICFPC 2005

Homepage of this year's contest.

Our current approximation of what the task will be is - cops and robbers. We should assume there will be two programs to be written, which will be fighting against each other.

What we will need with very high probablility:

  • cop program
  • robber program
  • engine for simulating the fight arena, which will be running those two programs as external processes (probably communicating by stdio)
  • visualisation for what is happenning on arena
  • others? tournament scripts, arena generators?
